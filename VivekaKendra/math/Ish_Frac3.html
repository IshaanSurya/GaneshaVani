Fractions 3
  Equalizing the denominators (dr) of  fractions. Steps...

Steps
1) Multiply (nr and dr ) of the each fraction with the   dr  of the other fractions. 
2) Step 1 will equalize all the dr
3) Since the dr are same, nr can be summed
4) The result is a fraction with summed up nr and equalized dr.
See Example
