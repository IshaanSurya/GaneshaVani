Verbs - Helper or Auxiliary - 1
  Helping verbs are used as number or tense or mood specifiers.
They are generally used along with a main verb.
Below is a list of commonly used helper-verbs
Helper Verb
 Other forms of it.
(* generally used as a plural)
Be am, is, was, *are, *were
*Have has, had
*Do does, did

Usage Eg:
Did we eat rotis for lunch?
We had packed rotis for lunch.
He was having rotis for lunch.
He is a farmer.
